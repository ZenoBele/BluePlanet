About Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver clean, reliable, and affordable LPG energy to every community — while maintaining the highest standards of safety, transparency, and environmental care.

Our Vision

To become South Africa’s most trusted name in sustainable LPG energy solutions, using innovation like smart gas monitoring, automated deliveries, and ongoing safety education to empower families, schools, and businesses across the country.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We deliver what we promise — transparent pricing, honest communication, and responsible service.

Safety First

Every cylinder, installation, and delivery is handled with professional safety checks and training.

Community

We build lasting relationships with local schools, businesses, and households — not just customers.

Innovation

We adopt smart gas monitoring systems and digital tools to modernize the energy experience.

Reliability

Your gas arrives on time, every time — because we understand consistency powers your life.

Peace

When you got us you get peace of mind, knowing you are going to cook today.
